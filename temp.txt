As the leader of Canada, I strongly believe in the importance of balancing the economy with environmental sustainability. While we recognize the importance of the oil and gas sector to the Canadian economy, we must also prioritize the transition towards cleaner and greener technologies.

To this end, our government has taken steps to phase out coal-fired electricity, invest in renewable energy, and implement a federal carbon pricing system. We have also put in place measures to ensure that Canada's oil and gas resources are developed responsibly and sustainably
